Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide when creating an account, such as your full name, email address, and student/staff ID. We also collect usage data to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to:

  • Manage your registrations and dashboard.
  • Communicate important event updates.
  • Verify event attendance and eligibility.
  • Improve our platform's layout and functionality.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your information is only shared with event organizers and college administrators for the purpose of managing the specific events you register for.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.
