Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?
To register for an event, sign in as a student, navigate to the 'Events' section on your dashboard, find the event you're interested in, and click 'Register Now'. Follow the prompts to complete your registration.
How can I host my own event?
If you are a club lead or organizer, sign in using your Organizer account. On the Organizer Hub, use the 'Create Event' form. Your event will be sent to the College Administrator for hall and date approval.
What is the difference between a free and paid event?
Free events allow for instant registration without any payment proof. Paid events require you to upload a screenshot of your payment (QR code or Bank Transfer) which will then be verified by the organizer.
How do I mark my attendance?
During the event, organizers will provide a 6-digit attendance code. Enter this code in the 'Attendance' section of your Student Hub to officially mark your presence.
